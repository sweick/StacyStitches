About Stacy


Born and raised in Central Illinois, I have always enjoyed sewing. I started sewing in Junior High School. After I married my wonderful husband, Greg, I began making clothes for our children. Now they are grown, and I expanded into repair work. Most of my clients are friends and relatives. Gratefully, my clientele has increased through referrals and word of mouth.

Please call or text me, and I will be pleased to assist you in any way I can.
